NYC Bus Performance

Schedule Adherence

A bus is considered "on time" if it departs a bus stop no later than five minutes after and no earlier than one minute before the time advertised at the stop. Select Bus Services are not included because their printed schedules are frequency-based (at least all the ones I've seen).

Actual departure times are inferred from data captured from the MTA Bus Time API. There is a GitHub project for analyzing the captured data. Resultant performance data are also available to download. Feedback and contributions are welcome – especially visualizations of the data!

The data are not maintained or endorsed by the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) and are not guaranteed to be accurate, complete, or timely. Please direct questions, comments, suggestions, and contributions to Nathan Johnson (nathan9@gmail.com).
